Audhumla Burgers! A little viking's favorite

What is it?

A simple classic that's easy to make and loved by all ages!

Ingredients and Steps

  • 1 lb of ground beef
  • onions
  • salt
  • pepper
  • buns
  • lettuce
  • tomatos
  • Ketchup/Mustard/relish/your condiment of choice
  1. Wash your hands before handling the ingrediets!
  2. Peel, decore, and then dice the onions.
  3. Knead the onions into the ground beef. Make sure they're evenly distibuted throughout
  4. knead a tsp of salt and pepper to the beef
  5. Portion out the beef into patties. The size and thickness is up to you
  6. With a slicked pan, cook the burgers on a setting of 5 (or whatever setting you're used too)
  7. Once patties are finished, place them on the buns
  8. Add vegtables of your choice
  9. Add condiments of your choice
  10. Enjoy!
