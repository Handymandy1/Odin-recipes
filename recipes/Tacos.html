tacos :3c

What is it?

An amazing finger food with a crunch more satisfying then your mace against your enemies skull!

Ingredients and Steps

  • Taco shells
  • One pound of ground beef
  • Taco seasoning
  • Lettuce
  • 2 Tomatoes
  • Shredded cheese
  • sour cream
  1. Cook the beef on a setting of 5 (or whatever setting you know works best for your stove)
  2. Add the taco seasoning during as the beef is being cooked
  3. Place the taco shells in the oven for 10 minutes
  4. While the meat is cooking, dice your tomatoes and shred your lettuce
  5. Once the meat and shells are done, begin making your tacos!
  6. Add shredded cheese, lettuce, tomatoes, and sour cream at your own discretion
